1. Introduction

Welcome to ImagenAI. This Privacy Policy describes how ImagenAI ("we", "us", or "our") handles your information when you use our macOS application.

2. Information Collection

Privacy is our priority. We do not collect, store, or transmit any personal identification information (PII) to our servers. All sensitive data, including but not limited to:

  • API Keys for third-party providers
  • User-entered Prompts
  • Drafted Images
  • App Settings

Are stored strictly on your local device using standard macOS secure storage methods.

3. Third-Party Services

ImagenAI provides integration with third-party AI service providers (such as OpenAI, Google Gemini, OpenRouter, and xAI). When you use these services:

  • Your prompts and reference images are sent directly to the respective service provider's API.
  • This data is subject to the privacy policies and terms of service of those providers. We encourage you to review their policies.

4. Local Processing

When using local AI models, all image generation and text processing occur entirely on your local hardware. No data is sent to external servers in this mode.

5. Data Retention

All data remains on your device until you choose to delete it or uninstall the application. We have no access to your data and cannot recover it if lost.

6. Changes to This Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page.
